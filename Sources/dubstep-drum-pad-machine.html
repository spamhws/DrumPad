Do you like to play Dubstep? Now it's easier than ever!

All you need is imagination and Dubstep Drum Pad Machine.
Play Dubstep rhythms on your iOs device, practice, compose your own songs, make records and surprise your friends! All in one App!

Dubstep Drum Pad Machine is a simple, easy and powerful tool for creating music on your mobile device. It combines the advantages of such instruments as the drum pad and drum machine. Ideal for playing in real time, as well as to create and play loops.

Professionally designed samples allow getting excellent results immediately without special training. The preset rhythms and tutorials make playing easier and faster.

Features:
- Professional music samples in the Dubstep style
- Sequencer for creating loops
- Editable preset rhythm for each sample pack
- Adjust tempo
- Functional design, which helps to quickly orient in pads
- Record your own tracks
- Video tutorials and tips for quick learning
